A college is an educational institution where people pursue higher learning and earn academic degrees.

It can be a standalone institution or part of a larger university, offering various programs like vocational training, associate degrees, or bachelor's degrees. Colleges play a vital role in personal and intellectual development, fostering critical thinking, and preparing individuals for careers and further academic pursuits
