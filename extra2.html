A hospital is a place where people receive medical care and treatment. It is equipped with doctors, nurses, and other healthcare professionals who work together to help patients recover from illnesses or injuries. Modern hospitals use advanced technology and equipment to diagnose and treat patients efficiently. They also offer services like laboratory testing, X-rays, and physiotherapy.
